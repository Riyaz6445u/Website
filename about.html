Our Mission

GEMINI EDU ID Card Generator was created to simplify the process of creating professional student ID cards for educational institutions. We believe that every student deserves a high-quality, professional-looking ID card that represents their institution with pride.

What We Do

Our platform provides an intuitive, user-friendly interface for creating custom ID cards. Whether you're a school administrator, college registrar, or university official, our tool helps you generate professional ID cards in minutes, not hours.
